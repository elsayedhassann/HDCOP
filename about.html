About Us

Who HDCOP

HDCOP for Engineering is a specialized engineering company founded in Egypt on 2022 , offering innovative, sustainable, and high-efficiency solutions in the water and wastewater treatment sector. We are proud to contribute to cleaner water and a healthier environment.

Our Vision

Egypt’s lifeblood, the Nile, provides over 90% of its freshwater. Yet, the country faces growing water challenges: • Scarcity: With only 560 cubic meters of water per person annually, Egypt is below the global water poverty line. • Pollution: Urbanization and agriculture contribute to water contamination. • Climate Change: Rising temperatures and erratic rainfall further strain resources. Despite these challenges, Egypt is embracing innovative solutions like modern irrigation, desalination, and wastewater treatment to secure its water future.

Our MISSION

At HDCOP, we’re proud to support these efforts with cutting-edge water treatment technologies, ensuring clean, sustainable water for all.
